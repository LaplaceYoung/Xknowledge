How to Export X Bookmarks to Obsidian

Use X-knowledge to turn saved tweets into searchable, reusable markdown assets.

Quick Nav

TL;DR Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 FAQ
TL;DR

In under 10 minutes, you can capture bookmarks, run AI enrichment, and export markdown into your Obsidian vault.

Step 1: Install extension and enable bookmark capture

  1. Load the extension in Chrome developer mode.
  2. Open your X bookmarks page to ingest tweet content.
  3. Confirm author and bookmark data are indexed.

Step 2: Generate summaries and tags in batch

  1. Configure your AI provider and model.
  2. Start batch processing and monitor success/failure.
  3. Retry failed tasks to keep metadata complete.

Step 3: Export markdown into your vault

  1. Choose Markdown or ZIP export.
  2. Organize files by date or topic folders.
  3. Build Obsidian queries and dashboards.

FAQ

  • Can it work offline? Yes. Data stays local by default and AI runs on demand.
  • How to avoid duplicate export? Filter by time range or tags before exporting.
